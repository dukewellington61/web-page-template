Portfolio Concept

I want my portfolio site to show the skills that I have and the work that I've done so far as a web developer. I want a no non-sense layout to display everything that matters in a straight forward, cool looking and minimalistic fashion.

Project Goals

My aim is to build up a portfolio in web development in a rapid fashion. Therefore I tread this course like a full time job. That means I will spend 40 hours a week on it and if necessary more. I have 3 month in total to complete training so the 9th of March 2019 is my deadline.

Course Goals

My goal is to acquire a solid background in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and Ruby on Rails so I can work as a freelancer in the exciting field of web development.

My Story

I have a degree in geology and I've been working in this field for many years both in the private and in the public sector. At some point I decided that it is time for a change so I headed east and worked as a teacher in Ho Chi Minh City. I moved on from there to become a tour guide for an off-road motorcyle adventure travel company in Cambodia. Later on I helped friends to set up a guesthouse in the lovely Cambodian town of Kampot which mainly involved construction work and gardening. Now it's time to do something completely different: Web Development
